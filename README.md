Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Chameleon Color Pro (the “App”) handles information when you use it.

The App is designed to process photos locally on your Mac. We do not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share your personal data through the App.

Information We Collect:

No data is collected

Local Processing Only

All photo processing and color correction performed by the App happens locally on your machine.

Your images remain on your device unless you choose to export, move, back up, or share them yourself using your own system or third-party services.

We do not upload your photos or editing data to our servers because we do not operate any cloud processing for the App.

Data Storage

The App may store files, preferences, or temporary working data locally on your Mac as needed for normal functionality. This local data stays on your device and is not sent to us.

Examples may include:
	•	App preferences
	•	Temporary processing files
	•	Locally saved exports
	•	Presets or user-selected settings stored on device

No Accounts

The App does not require you to create an account.

No Third-Party Tracking

The App does not use third-party advertising SDKs or third-party tracking technologies to monitor your activity across apps or websites.

No Sale or Sharing of Data

Because we do not collect your personal data through the App, we do not sell or share your personal information.

Permissions

The App may request access to files, folders, or the photo library only when needed for features you choose to use, such as opening or saving images. This access is used solely to perform those actions on your device.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect any data including childrens data as well.

Security

Because processing occurs locally and we do not receive your photo data, the primary security of your files depends on the security of your own device, operating system, storage, and backups.

Third-Party Services

If you choose to export files to, open files from, or store files with third-party services such as Apple Photos, iCloud Drive, Dropbox, Google Drive, or similar tools, your use of those services is governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above.
